Brastin

Programming, Game & Software Design, VR, 3D Modelling and Art

Hello! :D I enjoy making games and software. My favourite area to work in is user experience design, and I love creating (or at least trying to create) visually stunning projects that are interactive but also a treat to look at. I'm a fan of customization, and this is reflected in my most recent projects. I'm not a fan of the recent trend of more and more control being taken away from the user, so if I as a programmer have control over something such as colour, I try and allow the user to have this control too.
